Our Story

Founded in 2013, Island Hopes Travels began with a passion for sharing the beauty, culture, and warmth of Sri Lanka with the world. From humble beginnings, we’ve grown into a trusted name in the travel industry — known for our top-level, friendly service and a personalized approach that makes every journey special.
What truly sets us apart is the way we treat our travelers — not just as customers, but as friends. With a team that genuinely cares, we craft unforgettable travel experiences filled with local charm, comfort, and adventure. Whether you're a solo traveler, couple, or family, we're here to turn your travel hopes into lasting memories.
