Cultural Triangle Express

Duration

4 Days / 3 Nights

Group Size

Private Tour (2-6 people)

Destinations

4 Cities

Rating

4.8/5 (24 reviews)

Tour Overview

This compact tour is perfect for travelers who want to experience Sri Lanka's cultural highlights in a short time. You'll visit the ancient rock fortress of Sigiriya, the sacred Temple of the Tooth in Kandy, and the magnificent Dambulla Cave Temples.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Airport to Pinnawala to Kandy

  • Arrival at Colombo Bandaranaike Airport
  • Transfer to Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage
  • Visit Herbal Garden
  • Kandy City tour and viewpoint
  • Overnight at Amaya Hills Hotel

Day 2: Kandy

  • Visit Temple of the Tooth Relic
  • Cultural show
  • Peradeniya Botanical Garden
  • Bahirawa Kanda (Big Buddha Statue)
  • Gem museum
  • Overnight at Amaya Hills Hotel

Day 3: Kandy to Sigiriya

  • Visit Sigiriya Lion Rock
  • Hiriwadunna village Safari
  • Wood Carving Factory visit
  • Minneriya National Park Jeep Safari
  • Overnight Stay at Amaya Lake Hotel

Day 4: Sigiriya to Airport

  • Visit Dambulla Golden Cave Temple
  • Dambulla city tour
  • Negombo city tour
  • Dinner at local restaurant
  • Transfer to airport or extend your stay
