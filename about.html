Need Help?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and travel in Sri Lanka.

How far in advance should I book my trip?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for peak season (December to April) to ensure availability at preferred accommodations. For other times, 1-3 months is usually sufficient. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible, but options may be limited.

What's included in your tour packages?

Our packages typically include accommodation, transportation, guide services, and some meals/activities as specified. We can also arrange flights, travel insurance, and special experiences upon request. Each package is customized, so inclusions vary based on your preferences.

Can you accommodate special dietary requirements?

Absolutely. Sri Lankan cuisine is diverse and can accommodate most dietary needs. Just let us know about any restrictions (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, allergies, etc.) when booking, and we'll ensure all restaurants and accommodations are informed.

What's your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the services booked. Generally, deposits are refundable up to 60 days before travel, with decreasing percentages as the departure date approaches. We strongly recommend travel insurance to protect against unforeseen circumstances.

Is Sri Lanka safe for travelers?

Sri Lanka is generally very safe for travelers. We take extra precautions with transportation partners, accommodations, and guides to ensure your safety. Our 24/7 support line is always available if you need assistance during your trip.

Can you arrange special experiences like weddings or proposals?

Yes! We specialize in creating memorable moments. Whether it's a beachfront wedding, romantic proposal, anniversary celebration, or special family gathering, we'll handle all the details to make it magical.
